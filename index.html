Michael Tu

I am a Master's student in the David R. Cheriton School of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo, specializing at the intersection of natural language processing and information retrieval, advised by Professor Jimmy Lin. I also completed my undergrad at uWaterloo majoring in Software Engineering, graduating on the Dean's Honour's List in June 2017.

My interest is in natural language processing / text, applied machine learning, and building the infrastructure to support these goals. During my undergrad, I have done various internships focusing on various aspects of these responsibilities at Facebook, Uber, and Reflektion. One of my main strengths is I am skilled in building end-to-end systems, from the data pipelines in the backend to the clients in the frontend.

My overarching goals have remained the same for many years: to be an earnest person whom can be relied upon and to never stop learning. I enjoy photography, travelling, reading, and expanding my technical breadth and depth by taking online courses.
